The argument

AI doesn't give you data. It gives you a summary of a summary — text trained on descriptions of the record, filtered through an engagement-optimized search index, then further filtered by human raters who had no more access to the original than you do.

The federal record exists. The DOT files every flight delay. The FDA records every adverse event. The EPA tracks every contaminant violation. Most people never see it — not because it's hidden, but because no one built an interface to it.
